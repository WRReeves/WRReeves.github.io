About

The UT Undergraduate Algebraic Geometry Seminar is intended to be an introduction to algebraic geometry for an undergraduate audience. The seminar will start from the basics and focus on concrete examples and geometric intuition.

We will meet on Mondays and Fridays from 5 to 6 pm in PMA 9.166, starting on Friday, September 6th. Contact me for more information!
